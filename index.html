A Complete Guide to the Emirates Islamic RTA Card: Benefits, Features, and How to Apply

The Emirates Islamic RTA card is an innovative solution designed for residents and visitors in the UAE to conveniently access public transportation services. In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the benefits, features, and application process of the Emirates Islamic RTA card, helping you understand how it works and how you can make the most of it for your daily commuting needs.

What is the Emirates Islamic RTA Card?

The Emirates Islamic RTA card is a collaboration between Emirates Islamic Bank and the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) of Dubai. It serves as both a payment method for public transport and a prepaid card for various services. This card can be used to pay for Metro, bus, tram, and water taxi rides, among other services offered by RTA, making it a versatile and convenient tool for everyday travel across Dubai.

Key Features of the Emirates Islamic RTA Card

  • Multi-Purpose Use: The Emirates Islamic RTA card is not just limited to transport. It integrates with various RTA services such as parking fees, paying for taxis, and even purchasing tickets for events.
  • Ease of Use: The card operates using the NFC (Near Field Communication) technology, meaning users can simply tap the card at the RTA gates or kiosks.
  • Reloadable and Prepaid: Users can load funds onto the card via mobile banking, ATM, or RTA kiosks, making it a practical choice for regular commuters.
  • Multiple Payment Options: The card offers several payment options such as pay-as-you-go or monthly subscriptions for unlimited travel on Metro or buses.
  • Rewards and Offers: Emirates Islamic customers can access exclusive discounts and offers for shopping, dining, and entertainment across Dubai.
  • Security and Protection: The card comes with an embedded chip and PIN security system, providing protection against unauthorized transactions.
  • Compatible with RTA Apps: Seamlessly integrated into the RTA Dubai app, allowing users to manage their balance, check transactions, and monitor travel history.

Benefits of the Emirates Islamic RTA Card

  • Convenience and Time-Saving: The card reduces the need for cash or paper tickets, allowing you to simply tap and go.

How to Apply for the Emirates Islamic RTA Card

Step 1: Eligibility Criteria

To apply for the Emirates Islamic RTA card, you must meet the following basic eligibility requirements:

  • Be a resident of the UAE or a visitor with valid documentation.
  • Have a valid Emirates ID or passport for identification.
  • Hold an Emirates Islamic Bank account (if applying through the bank) or visit an RTA service center for card registration.

Step 2: Apply Online or In-Person

You can apply for the Emirates Islamic RTA card in the following ways:

  • Online Application: Existing Emirates Islamic customers can apply through the mobile banking app or website.
  • In-Person: Non-bank customers can visit any RTA service center or sales kiosk located at Metro stations.

Step 3: Submit Required Documents

Submit the following documents for the application process:

  • A copy of your Emirates ID or passport.
  • Proof of residency or visitor visa (if applicable).
  • A recent photograph (if required).
  • Proof of Emirates Islamic bank account (if applying through the bank).

Step 4: Choose Your Plan and Load Funds

Once your application is approved, choose from different plans available, such as single-ride, daily, or monthly options. You can load funds via credit/debit cards, bank transfer, or by visiting an RTA kiosk.

Step 5: Start Using the Card

Once you have your card, you can start using it immediately for transportation and other RTA services.
